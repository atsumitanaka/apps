Key Points

  1. The next 7 days are critical: Between today (6/24) and the NIMS application opening (7/1), complete your Tsukuba eligibility self-assessment and (if necessary) send the pre-confirmation email.
  2. If you fail the NIMS document screening (early August): You can still apply to Tsukuba independently, but you will not receive NIMS-affiliated benefits. Adjust your Tsukuba preparation intensity accordingly.
  3. Special note for Requirement (6) applicants: The pre-confirmation email is separate from Eligibility Screening (8/25) and should be done earlier. Finish it in July so August can be dedicated to application document preparation.
  4. English test scores must arrive by the deadline: Official score reports for TOEIC / TOEFL / IELTS take 2–4 weeks to issue. If you have not yet taken the test, book it immediately.

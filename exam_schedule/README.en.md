## Step 0: Self-Assessment of Tsukuba Eligibility

Source: University of Tsukuba "On Graduate School Application Eligibility and Eligibility Screening" (t1-1.pdf)

### A. Applicants who do NOT require Eligibility Screening (Requirements 1–8)

| Status | Req. | Description | Required Action |
|---|---|---|---|
| ✅ | (1) | Graduated from a Japanese 4-year university (or expected by the month before enrollment) | **No screening** → proceed directly to application |
| ✅ | (2) | Granted a bachelor's degree by the National Institution for Academic Degrees and University Evaluation (or expected) | **No screening** → proceed directly to application |
| ✅ | (3) | Completed 16 years of school education abroad (or expected) — i.e., graduated from a foreign university whose program requires 16+ years from primary school to graduation | **No screening** → proceed directly to application |
| ✅ | (4) | Completed the same 16-year foreign curriculum via correspondence education taken in Japan (or expected) | **No screening** → proceed directly to application |
| ✅ | (5) | Completed the program of a MEXT-designated foreign university branch campus in Japan (or expected) | **No screening** → proceed directly to application |
| ⚠️ | (6) | Granted a bachelor's-equivalent degree by completing a foreign university program of **at least 3 years** (including courses shorter than 16 years total) | **No screening, BUT pre-confirmation by email is mandatory**<br>`admission.grad@un.tsukuba.ac.jp` |
| ✅ | (7) | Completed a MEXT-designated specialized training college (senshu-gakko) program (or expected) | **No screening** → proceed directly to application |
| ✅ | (8) | Designated by the Minister of MEXT (e.g., graduates of National Defense Academy, Fisheries University, Meteorological College; holders of an Advanced or Class-1 teaching license aged 22+) | **No screening** → proceed directly to application |

### B. Applicants who DO require Eligibility Screening (Requirements 9–11)

| Status | Req. | Description | Required Action |
|---|---|---|---|
| 🟥 | (9) | Individually screened by the graduate school and recognized as having academic ability equivalent to a university graduate, and aged 22+ | **Eligibility Screening required** |
| 🟥 | (10) | Enrolled in a university for 3+ years and recognized as having earned the prescribed credits with excellent grades | **Eligibility Screening required** |
| 🟥 | (11) | Completed a 15-year foreign curriculum (in-person or correspondence) and earned the prescribed credits with excellent grades | **Eligibility Screening required** |

> **Notes for applicants under (B)**
> - Required documents: (a) Personal Record (designated form), (b) Academic/Research/Work History Record (designated form), (c) Graduation certificate or equivalent proving studies after high school (copies acceptable), (d) Other documents required by the Academic Service Office
> - **Do NOT pay the examination fee until the screening result is issued.**
> - Mail submission: use a Kakugata-2 envelope (332mm × 240mm), registered express mail, must arrive within the application period.
> - Submission address & inquiries: https://www.tsukuba.ac.jp/admission/graduate-overview/shikaku/

## Key Points

1. **The next 7 days are critical:** Between today (6/24) and the NIMS application opening (7/1), complete your Tsukuba eligibility self-assessment and (if necessary) send the pre-confirmation email.
2. **If you fail the NIMS document screening (early August):** You can still apply to Tsukuba independently, but you will not receive NIMS-affiliated benefits. Adjust your Tsukuba preparation intensity based on the screening result.
3. **Special note for Requirement (6) applicants:** The pre-confirmation email is a separate procedure from Eligibility Screening (8/25) and should be completed earlier. Get the confirmation done in July so you can focus on application documents from August onward.
4. **English test scores must arrive by the application deadline:** Official score reports for TOEIC / TOEFL / IELTS take 2–4 weeks to issue. If you have not yet taken the test, book it immediately.
